About us

Ziyadat Bank - Islamic Bank based on Islamic finance principles and latest technologies

Who we are?

At Ziyadat Bank, we believe in a better way to bank—one that is fair, ethical, and transparent. Our financial solutions are built on the principles of Islamic finance, ensuring a riba-free (interest-free) and Sharia-compliant banking experience. By combining the wisdom of Islamic financial principles with cutting-edge AI technology, we empower individuals and businesses to manage their finances seamlessly and responsibly.

Our mission

To revolutionize the banking industry by providing accessible, ethical, and technology-driven financial solutions that align with Sharia principles and empower people to achieve financial success.

Our vision

To be a global leader in Islamic banking, fostering financial inclusion, innovation, and social responsibility through AI-powered solutions.

Our values

  • Sharia Compliance - 100% riba-free, ethical banking
  • Trust & transparency - Honest financial services with no hidden fees
  • Innovation & AI Integration - Smart banking solutions for efficiency and security
  • Social Responsibility - Investing in community-driven projects and charities

Our commitment to You

Ziyadat Bank is more than a financial institution — it’s a movement towards ethical, technology-driven finance. We are committed to providing financial solutions that respect your values, ensuring security, growth, and peace of mind.
