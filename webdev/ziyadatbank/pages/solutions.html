Ziyadat Bank Solutions - Empowering Individuals & Businesses

At Ziyadat Bank, we provide Sharia-compliant, AI-powered financial solutions that help individuals, startups, and businesses achieve financial success while staying true to ethical Islamic banking principles. Discover how our smart and transparent banking has transformed lives.

How We Help Individuals?

Personalized banking that aligns with your values.

  • Debt-Free Financing – Access interest-free personal loans for essential needs.
  • Zakat & Charity Management – Simplify donations with automated tools.
  • AI-Powered Savings Plans – Intelligent budgeting for better financial planning.

How We Help Startups?

Fueling entrepreneurship with ethical financial solutions.

Ethical business banking for sustainable growth.

  • Corporate Debit & Credit Cards – Secure transactions with AI fraud detection.
